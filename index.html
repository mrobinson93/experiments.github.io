Background

Hello, my name is Maria Robinson and I'm currently an NRSA postdoc fellow in Timothy Brady's lab at the University of California, San Diego.

I'm a cognitive psychologist, broadly interested in combining empirical and quantitatve methods towards understanding intelligent systems. To this end, I've used diverse analytic approaches to study human attention, memory and decision-making. Much of my interests also connect to topics on best practices in theory assessment and measurement in psychology.

I received my PhD in Psychology in 2019 at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. While at Illinois, I completed my dissertation project under the supervision of Aaron Benjamin and collaborated with David Irwin as well as Michel Regenwetter.
