Hello, my name is Maria M. Robinson and I'm currently a postdoc in Timothy F. Brady's lab at the University of California in San Diego.

I am broadly interested in combining behavioral methods and computational modeling to study visual cognition and memory.

I received my PhD in Psychology at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where I worked with Aaron S. Benjamin and collaborated with David E. Irwin as well as Michel Regenwetter.
